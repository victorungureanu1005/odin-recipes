Risotto

Description

Ingredients:

  • Arborio rice
  • Mushrooms (e.g., cremini or button)
  • Vegetable or chicken broth
  • Onion
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Saut√© chopped onion in olive oil.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and cook.
  3. Stir in Arborio rice until coated.
  4. Gradually add warm broth.
  5. Cook and stir until rice is tender.
  6. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
