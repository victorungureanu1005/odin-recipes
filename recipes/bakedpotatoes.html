Baked Potatoes

Description

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh herbs

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and cut potatoes into desired shape.
  3. Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes until crispy.
  6. Optional: Sprinkle fresh herbs before serving.
