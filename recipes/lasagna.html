Lasagna Recipe

Description

Incredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Brown ground beef or sausage.
  3. Layer noodles, meat, and cheese.
  4. Repeat for desired number of layers.
  5. Top with tomato sauce.
  6. Bake until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let cool before serving.
